# Welcome to ACTIA IME’s GitHub

ACTIA IME develops embedded systems, on-board electronics and vehicle diagnostics solutions.

With over 40 years of experience in Vehicle Communication Interfaces (VCIs), we support projects from concept to after-sales across a wide range of vehicle platforms.

ACTIA IME GmbH is part of the ACTIA Group, with 30 locations in 17 countries.  
Our main sites are in Braunschweig and Böblingen (Stuttgart), Germany.

Below you can find a selection of our open-source projects.  
All repositories are available [here](https://github.com/orgs/ACTIA-IME-GmbH/repositories).
